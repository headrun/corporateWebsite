About Us

Headrun believes deeply in creating a culture that allows innovation and quality to flourish.
This pervades the entire gamut of operations at Headrun from writing code, talking to customers to management methodology.

Efficiency

We constantly strive for efficiency in ourselves and in the systems we build. The image of an efficient self, other or system drives us forward. We constantly consider questions like "How quickly can I solve this problem?", "What tools do I employ, so I can get this done effortlessly?". We love the notion of a "Lazy programmer" - Do not reinvent the wheel!, Do not repeat yourself. We constantly challenge ourselves to spend less time at something but get more done. We reward employees for doing the same.

Love of expertise

Headrunner's are experts at what they do. We enjoy watching other experts in action. A batsman dispatching a perfect yorker to the stands, A shop keeper handling ten customers simultaneously, A Unix veteran working magic on the console, A mathematician expressing a model as an elegant equation - All of these set out pulse racing. We strive to set ourselves and each other new standards in expertise.

Quality

We love quality products that we use. We pay gladly for quality services. Headrun respects quality. We emphasise quality conciousness in all at Headrun. We take the thought "This is a product of Headrun. It has to set a new bar." quite seriously.

Empathy

Do unto others as you would have done to you; Our interactions with each other, the team with an individual, an individual with the team, Us with a client are all governed by this over arching thought. We care about the people around us. We believe that our life is more meaningful when we grow together. As product makers and service providers, we empathise with the customer. We think of ourselves as partners more than vendors.

Innovation

We always question Status Quo. Why is it this way? Can it not be done like this also? Should be re-evaluate this practise? Can I do it better? - We are not afraid to go into battle mode to bring about change having recognized that "Change is the only thing that is permanent".

Pride

At Headrun, we are all proud of the work we do. Any Headrunner will proudly showcase his work and immodestly and "unflatteredly" accept the incoming praise :) We know we are on a mission to change the world and that brings out the very best.

Fairness

Being a company does not mean we abandon the code of a gentleman. We give prime importance to fairness in our dealings with clients and our employees. We have systems in place to ensure that people are rewarded justly, and that there is no room for partisanship, favoritism. We strive to build an uncorruptible meritocracy.

Transparency

Our internal management and government systems are automated allowing and open allowing for greater transparency in operations for the benefit of our employees and clients alike.

Pragmatic idealism

Idealism is a long-term vision. Pragmatism is short-term common sense. At Headrun, we have found the "Nirvanic" balance between both.

No Fear

We constantly put ourselves in situations the equivalent of climbing Everest without an oxygen pack - Call it Headrun's version of military war games.

Simplicity, Minimalism and Frugality

Simple but not too simple is our mantra. We strive for simplicity in our thoughts and actions. We constantly fight for creation of order from chaos. Complexity is disorder, concordantly, Simplicity is order. The technology we choose, tools we use, business models we put in place, project planning we employ are all passed through our complexity filter. We constantly question "Is this complexity really required? Have we arrived at a simple and elegant solution yet?"
